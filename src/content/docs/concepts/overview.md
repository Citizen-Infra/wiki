Citizen infrastructure comes with its own vocabulary — *sortition*, *deliberative democracy*, *citizens' assemblies*, *wikisurveys*, and more. This section explains those methods and ideas in plain language: what each one is, how it works, and where to see it in practice.

It's a living reference that grows over time. Where a concept has real-world examples, it links out to the [Run Reports](/run-reports/overview/); where it connects to something you can use, it links to [the toolkit](/toolkit/overview/).

This section is just getting started — the first explainers are on their way. Spot one that's missing? Use the "Edit page" link below.
