This is a free, public resource about **citizen infrastructure** — the idea that
citizens can build and use technology to act together on shared problems, and a
growing toolkit of things you can actually use. You don't need to be a member of
anything to read it or use it.

## Who stewards it

It's published and maintained by the **Citizen Infrastructure Builders Club
(CIBC)** — a group of people building technology for collective action. This wiki
is the public-facing body of knowledge; the club itself lives elsewhere.

- **Join the club →** [cibc.notion.site](https://cibc.notion.site/)
- **Support the work →** [opencollective.com/citizen-infra](https://opencollective.com/citizen-infra)

## It's open

The whole resource is open and editable. Spot something wrong or missing? Use the
"Edit page" link at the bottom of any page, or browse the source on
[GitHub](https://github.com/Citizen-Infra/wiki).
